— My Story —

In 1996, I bought my first book on HTML. It was just a hobby. I never believed I would make a career out of it. It was the mid-90s, and the web was a very different place. Many people didn't take the web seriously, just as I didn't take web development seriously.

It was just a hobby

Those were simpler times. Font tags, color attributes, and table layouts were the norm, but that didn't last long.

The Web is ever-changing, and if you want to survive, you must change with it.

It would take 10 years before I was able to turn my hobby into a paycheck. I finally started taking it seriously. Since that time, I have understood how crucial it is to constantly be learning and improving my skills. I have learned the hard way how relying on yesterday's achievements today, means I will be left behind tomorrow. The Web is ever-changing, and if you want to survive you must change with it.

New Country. New Challenge. New Chance.

In August 2014, I moved from the States to Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. After 7 years with the same company, I was ready for something new. A chance to seek out new challenges, new careers and new opportunities. I am ambitious, a self starter, quick to learn and motivated. I have over 8 years of professional experience. I specialize in front-end development with knowledge in HTML5, CSS3, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS, Sencha, PHP, CodeIgnitor, and Laravel.
